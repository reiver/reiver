§ CP437

The CP437 character-set is a 128-bit character-set that was common with the IBM PC and the IBM PC clones.

Other names for CP437 include:

• csPC8CodePage437,
• code-page-437,
• dos-latin-us,
• oem-437,
• oem-us, and
• pc-8.

All CP437 characters have equivalents in the unicode character-set; although that numberical representations of the characters do different with many of them.
